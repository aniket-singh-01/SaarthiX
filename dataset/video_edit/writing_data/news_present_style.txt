Short tech news presentation method:


Key requirments:

1. third-person narrative
2. Don't write too short sentences (Each sentence should contain more than 11 words)
3. Do not use Arabic numerals. Change all numbers to English words, such as 5 becomes five.
4. Do not write false or non-existent digital data, for example, do not write iPhone 15 as iPhone 15.0
5. When encountering abbreviations of proper nouns, separate the word and the abbreviation appropriately, for example, ChatGPT becomes Chat GPT, OpenAI becomes Open AI, and AndroidOS becomes Android OS
6. Understand the main characters of the news content, for example, Apple released the iPhone 4, not the iPhone 4 released a new product.
7. Do not use slash '/' within the writing

###################### The opening part: the first three sentences are very attractive, quickly point the core gimmick, don't say something like good mornings/afternoon/evenings...

Content part (accounting for most of the copy): according to the news events to promote the plot timeline unfolding, the main line is clear, constantly advancing the plot, do not generalize the events and characters, appropriate use of key characters in the dialogue, the ending part (the penultimate sentence): refining the deeper meaning of the news, sublimation theme

Tight pacing, smooth scene transitions, constant plot advancement, and strong characterization.

The final copy should not mention words such as “the end of the news” or “the beginning of the news” that make the audience feel out of place.

Strictly no analytical reflections or thematic summaries, no macro-summaries that do not summarize the theme or character development, and strictly no use of the phrase: “The news shows how...”. ; showing the... ; episodes... ; shows the... ; reveals... ; reveals the story of... Generalized words.

The word 'news' should not be mentioned in the body of the text.

Third-person perspective

The first three sentences of a news flash copywriter are a summary of the content, and after 2-3 sentences of introduction, the story begins.

###################### Body content section: unfold according 

To the plot timeline, with a clear main line

Literary analysis, reflection and summary of themes are strictly prohibited

Please do not give a macro-summary if it is not an overview of the theme of the presentation or the development of the characters

The use of the following is strictly prohibited: by... ; showing the... ; plot... ; demonstrates... ; reveals... ; reveals the presentation of... Generalized words.

Do not mention the word 'news' in the body of the text.

Use common words, e.g. “really”, "and", "how", "if", "because", "but" etc.

Advance the plot to maintain narrative momentum

###################### Closing section:

Refine the deeper meaning of the news, sublimate the theme, don't highly summarize content

Last sentence requirments:

prompt audience to follow this account or social media (eg. If you like the video content we create, please follow our account and there will be more relevant information later)
